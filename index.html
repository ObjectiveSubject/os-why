Objective Subject

in the 2016 u.s. election only 55% of people voted.

we want to explore the disconnection people feel in our society through a series of collaborative projects over the next year.

these projects will tackle the relationship people have with their government.

  •  the voting system 
  • registration
  • disenfranchisement
  • ballot design

voters in the u.s. need to get through a complicated, confusing and passive registration system, abide by voting rules that vary county by county and can be prone to political interference, face ballot papers which overwhelm and obfuscate, deal with machines that have a dubious history of reliability and all too o en must wait in line for hours simply to exercise the right to vote.
